Although science-based conservation is at the heart of Audubon's mission to protect birds and habitats, we know that communities also play an essential role in climate change adaptation or mitigation efforts. This map features climate-related events, such as wildfires and salmon die-offs, from sources including the US Fish and Wildlife Service, University of Alaska- Fairbanks, and National Marine Fisheries Service, but it is also designed to accept user submissions from everyday Alaskans. Have you noticed climate change affecting your community? We want to hear from you! You can find detailed information on adding your own submissions on the "Help" tab.
